Senyro

Senyro - Elevate Roleplay, Unlock New Worlds.

Senyro is an innovative web application, created and led by a 15-year-old developer from Germany, aiming to elevate roleplay for everyone. Senyro opens up new worlds, streamlines complex processes, and delivers unique experiences for every community.

Features

Community Creation & Discovery

Build and manage your own roleplay community directly on Senyro. Invite your players, customize every detail, or make your world discoverable through our public Discovery Page. Communities are made by players, for players — Senyro provides the framework, you shape the experience.

Character & ID Management

Players can create and manage detailed characters and ID cards within each community. Community owners can optionally review and approve character submissions for realism and consistency. Total flexibility — toggle this system on or off at any time.

Faction System

Empower structured roleplay with customizable factions. Create ranks, assign roles, promote or demote members — all controlled by the faction owner. It's a decentralized system designed for realism and immersion.

Integrated Banking System

Every character has a personal bank account — earn, spend, and manage finances in-game. Factions also have shared accounts for salaries or operations. The entire banking module can be enabled or disabled per community.

More features are already in development - Senyro evolves with your roleplay.
